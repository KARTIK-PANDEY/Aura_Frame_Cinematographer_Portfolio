Our Philosophy

“The most beautiful films are the ones that make you feel.”

Aura & Frame is a boutique creative studio crafting luxury wedding films and editorial photography for couples worldwide. Inspired by the romance of vintage cinema, raw emotion, and high fashion, we capture candid milestones and refine them into timeless works of art. We believe every couple has a signature soundtrack, a distinct light, and a story waiting to be told.

Cinematic Art

Crafting The Layers of Memory

Filmmaking is more than recording frames—it's a symphony of pacing, lighting, and composition. We compose unique soundscapes for every film and grade our colors by hand, echoing classic celluloid aesthetics. Every look, every sigh, and every burst of laughter is preserved in high-fidelity emotion.
